# Policy Review Notes

All policy pages in the initial website are serious placeholders and must be reviewed by
the owner and legal counsel before launch.

Required review topics:

- Perishable fresh mushroom pickup windows and missed pickups
- Local delivery boundaries and customer availability
- Shelf-stable shipping timing, carrier delays, and damaged packages
- Supplement labeling, structure/function language, and required FDA disclaimer
- Refund limits for perishable goods
- Customer data handling and email signup consent
- Stripe or other payment processor terms
- Future analytics/cookie disclosure
